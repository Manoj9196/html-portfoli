The Best Movies in Kannada According To Manoj in last 5 years

The Top 5 Movies Are:

1. KGF Chapter 1 and 2

I love this movie for it's emotional elements, best in class cinematography with dark shades and best captivating action music.
Especsially the way Yash has acted and Prashant Neel directed and narrated it without getting bored anywhere.

2. Kantara

The divine experience of the coastal Karnataka, and Rishab Shetty's performance as an actor and director is just mindblowing.
The music, and cinematography makes me fall in love with the coastal Karnataka's culture and nature

3. Sapthasagaradache Yello Side A and B

Directed by P Hemanth Rao, this romantic and tragic love story shows how one should love.
And also not to fall for any traps that could ruin your life. Rakshit Shetty's acting is top notch, he proves how versatile he is in every film.
His expressions, especially the fight sequences are really awesome.

4. 777 Charlie

A tale of love for pet, from being dog hater to dog lover and who does anything to make his dog happy.
Rakshit shetty, not only is a good director/or story writer, but also a fantastic actor, he proves in all of his movies.
Especially this movie, his acting will definetly make you cry. This movie should be shown to everyone, so that they can be kind to animals

5. Ibbani Tabbida Ileyali

Produced by Rakshit Shetty and written and directed by one of his close associates who was a part 7 Odds directors team Chandrashaker.
I rarely watch romantic or love story movies, but this was well crafted and every frame was an art.
The characters are well written, and the cinematography is really like a painting in every frame. Music is soulful. One of the best and well executed love story in recent times.
